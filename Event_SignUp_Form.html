Yoga Benefits

  1. Improved Flexibility: Regular yoga practice helps increase your range of motion and flexibility.
  2. Enhanced Strength: Yoga poses help build muscle strength and endurance.
  3. Stress Relief: Yoga techniques promote relaxation and reduce stress levels.
  4. Better Posture: Yoga helps improve your posture and alignment.

Yoga History

  • Yoga originated in ancient India over 5,000 years ago.
  • Traditional Clothing: Sarees, salwar kameez, and kurta pajamas are popular traditional attire in India.
  • Music and Dance: Classical music and dance forms like Bharatanatyam, Kathak, and Odissi are an integral part of Indian culture.
  • Yoga and Meditation: India is the birthplace of yoga and meditation, which are practiced worldwide for physical and mental well-being.
